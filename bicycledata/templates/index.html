Rural Cycling in Focus

Welcome to our platform dedicated to collecting and analysing empirical data on rural cycling! We provide the TrafficLogger, a modular data collection device, which can be attached to bikes and other modes of transportation. It samples and uploads data like location, speed and overtaking distance from your trips. Our automated and custom post-processing methods help understand the situation of active travellers on rural roads and provide empirical results on which improvements can be based. Please get in touch if you are interested in launching a project with us.
